About Me

I received both my Bachelor's and Ph.D. degrees from the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC). I am currently a professor in the School of Computer Science at Fudan University. I was awarded the honor of China Outstanding Young Scholar.

My research interests include steganography, watermarking, forensics, and AI security. I have published more than 200 peer-reviewed papers in journals such as IEEE T-PAMI, T-IFS, T-DSC, T-IP, T-MM, T-CYB, T-CSVT, T-CC, and T-ASLP, and the conferences like CVPR, ICCV, NeurIPS, AAAI, IJCAI, ACM MM, CIKM, ECAI, ICME, ICASSP, etc. I have also published three books, esp., one of which is about China history for children.

I serve as the Associate Editor for IEEE T-CYB, IEEE T-CSVT, SP, and JVCI. I have also served as the Area Chair for ICME 2023, the Industry Chair for WIFS 2022, the Session Chair for MMSP 2022, and the Area Chair for ChinaMFS.
